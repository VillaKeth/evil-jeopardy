# 🎂 Evil Jeopardy 1.2 — How to Play the Baking Games

Your virtual cake journey has **6 phases**. Each phase is a minigame. Some phases have a wild "absurd" variant that might randomly appear instead. Here's how to survive them all.

---

## Phase 1: PREP — Measure the Ingredients
**⏱ 75 seconds**

You have surgeon-simulator hands. Measure 5 ingredients into the mixing bowl.

| Control | Action |
|---------|--------|
| **Mouse** | Move your hand around the counter |
| **A S D F G** | Curl each finger (thumb → pinky) |
| **Space** | Switch between left/right hand |

**How to play:**
1. Move your hand near the glowing ingredient container
2. Curl 3+ fingers (e.g. hold S + D + F) to grip it
3. While holding, move the mouse up/down to tilt and pour
4. Watch the fill gauge on the right — stop at the green zone
5. Release fingers to drop the container and lock in your score
6. Repeat for all 5 ingredients (flour, sugar, eggs, butter, milk)

**Scoring:** Accuracy of each pour vs. the target amount. Closer = more points.

---

## Phase 2: MIX — Stir the Batter
**⏱ 50 seconds**

Draw circles with your mouse to stir. Match the target speed.

| Control | Action |
|---------|--------|
| **Mouse (circular motion)** | Stir the bowl |

**How to play:**
1. Move your mouse in circles inside the bowl
2. Watch the speed meter on the right — stay in the green zone
3. 3 rounds of increasing speed:
   - **Round 1:** Slow Fold
   - **Round 2:** Steady Beat  
   - **Round 3:** Power Whip
4. Too slow = "FASTER!" / Too fast = "TOO FAST!"

**Scoring:** Time spent in the green speed zone per round. Max 33 pts each.

### 🐄 Absurd Variant: COW COMBAT
**⏱ 50 seconds**

You're milking a cow. Click the glowing udder on the beat. Dodge attacks.

| Control | Action |
|---------|--------|
| **Click** | Squeeze the glowing udder |
| **DODGE button** | Avoid cow charges |

**How to play:**
1. One udder glows green every 0.8 seconds — click it!
2. Wrong udder = penalty. Missed beat = penalty.
3. The cow attacks every ~8 seconds:
   - **🦵 KICK** — Don't click anything, hands off!
   - **🐂 CHARGE** — Hit the DODGE button fast!
   - **🌀 SPIN** — Wait it out, udders are scrambled
4. At 30 seconds: **STAMPEDE** — 3 mini cows charge, dodge each one!

**Scoring:** +5 per successful milk, -2 per miss. Fill the bucket!

## Phase 3: BAKE — Control the Oven
**⏱ 45 seconds**

Keep the temperature in the target zone. Don't burn it!

| Control | Action |
|---------|--------|
| **+ button** (hold) | Increase temperature |
| **- button** (hold) | Decrease temperature |

**How to play:**
1. The target temperature shifts over time
2. Hold +/- to adjust your oven temp to match
3. Stay within ±14° of target for best score
4. Too hot for too long = cracks on the cake

**Scoring:** Closeness to target × speed bonus. Cracks reduce score by 10% each.

### 🏎 Absurd Variant: RACING OVEN
**⏱ 45 seconds**

Your oven is on a racetrack. Dodge obstacles, collect orbs.

| Control | Action |
|---------|--------|
| **A/D or ←/→** | Switch lanes (3 lanes) |
| **Space** | Jump over obstacles |

**How to play:**
1. Your oven races forward automatically
2. Switch between 3 lanes to avoid kitchen hazards
3. Collect glowing orbs for bonus points (+10 each)
4. Crashing costs -15 points

## Phase 4: COOL — Cool the Cake
**⏱ 40 seconds**

Fan the cake to cool it from 100° to 25°. Don't crack it!

| Control | Action |
|---------|--------|
| **Click + hold** | Activate the fan |
| **Mouse movement** | Aim the fan at the cake |

**How to play:**
1. Hold click to turn on the fan
2. Move mouse to position the fan near the cake
3. Closer = stronger cooling, but **too close for too long = CRACKS**
4. Fan intensity > 80% for 2+ seconds = crack appears
5. Watch the cake color: Red → Orange → Green (target) → Blue

**Scoring:** Temperature closeness × speed bonus. -10% per crack.

### 💎 Absurd Variant: JEWEL SORT
**⏱ 40 seconds**

Sort colored gems from a conveyor belt into matching vats.

| Control | Action |
|---------|--------|
| **Click gem** | Pick it up |
| **Click vat** | Drop into matching color vat |

**How to play:**
1. Gems roll along a conveyor belt (left to right)
2. Click a gem to grab it
3. Click the matching color vat (red/blue/green/yellow)
4. Gems that reach the end are lost (-2 pts)
5. Belt speeds up as you progress!

**Scoring:** +5 correct, -3 wrong, -2 missed.

## Phase 5: DECORATE — Make It Pretty
**⏱ 60 seconds**

Frost, top, and fondant your cake. Get creative!

| Control | Action |
|---------|--------|
| **Left click** | Paint frosting dots |
| **Left drag** | Draw frosting lines |
| **Right drag** | Rotate the cake |
| **Tool buttons** | Switch: Frosting / Toppings / Fondant |
| **Color buttons** | Pick frosting color |

**How to play:**
1. Select a tool (Frosting is default)
2. Pick a color and paint on the cake surface
3. Switch to Toppings to place strawberries, blueberries, sprinkles, chocolate chips
4. Apply Fondant for a smooth layer (+8 neatness bonus)
5. Use multiple colors for creativity bonus
6. Place at least 10 items for max accuracy score

**Scoring:** Accuracy (items placed) + Creativity (colors used) + Neatness (no overlaps + fondant).

### 🔄 Absurd Variant: GRAVITY FLIP
**⏱ 45 seconds**

Your cake is on a rack. Gravity keeps flipping. Keep it stable!

| Control | Action |
|---------|--------|
| **Click the cake** | Anchor it in place (1.5s hold, 3s cooldown) |

**How to play:**
1. Every 4-6 seconds, gravity flips to a random direction
2. Click the cake to anchor it before it slides off
3. Furniture flies around — watch out!
4. Don't overuse anchors (penalty for using more than 10)

**Scoring:** 100 base - damage + speed bonus + anchor efficiency bonus.

## Phase 6: PRESENT — The Horror Maze
**⏱ 240 seconds (4 minutes)**

Carry your cake through a 14-room horror maze. Reach the demonic judges alive.

| Control | Action |
|---------|--------|
| **W/A/S/D or Arrows** | Walk / Strafe |
| **Mouse** | Look around (pointer lock) |
| **Space** | Dodge attacks |

**How to play:**
1. First-person view — you're carrying the cake
2. Navigate through 14 rooms, each with different hazards
3. When attacked, press **Space** to dodge (QTE timing!)
4. Your cake has a health bar — every hit damages it
5. Demonic judges chase you in the final rooms
6. Reach the end with as much cake integrity as possible

**Scoring:** Final cake health percentage (0-100).

### 🏃 Absurd Variant: OBSTACLE COURSE
**⏱ 50 seconds**

Sprint through a kitchen corridor dodging hazards.

| Control | Action |
|---------|--------|
| **W/A/S/D or Arrows** | Move / Strafe |
| **Space** | Slide under obstacles |

**Hazard types:** Swinging rolling pins, falling pots, butter slicks, steam vents.

**Scoring:** 100 base - hazard penalties + integrity bonus + speed bonus + end-of-course bonus.

## 💡 Tips

- **Shop items matter!** Buy an electric mixer (easier MIX), oven thermometer (easier BAKE), food coloring (more DECORATE colors), or fondant (neatness bonus)
- **Chaos events** can happen at any time — butter fingers, earthquakes, inverted controls. Roll with it!
- **The absurd variants** are random — you might get normal MIX or COW COMBAT. Embrace the chaos.
- **Score feeds into judging** — your virtual baking score affects the taste/accuracy dimensions when the host judges your team's cake
